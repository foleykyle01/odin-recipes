Lemon Chicken Orzo Soup

About the Soup:

The Lemon Chicken Orzo soup from this recipe is another hit with the girlfriend. This soup
is great in the dead of winter and in the ealry spring as it can be served both hot and warm

This soup does not cook in a crock pot making the cooking a bit more involved but much less
time over all. The other benifit is that the recipe uses a rotissere chicken which is super easy!

Time to Make:

50 mins

Ingredents:

  • 2 sticks celery chopped finely
  • 2 medium carrots peeled & chopped finely
  • 1/2 medium onion chopped
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Box and a half of chicken broth
  • 1/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 rotissere chicken
  • 1 cup uncooked orzo
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley or to taste
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Saut√© the celery, carrots, and onions in a large soup pot with the butter and oil over medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Stir in the garlic and cook for about 30 seconds, then add the flour and cook for another minute or so.
  3. Pour in chicken broth and stir until the flour has dissolved, then add the Italian seasoning and chicken. Bring the soup to a boil.
  4. Cover the soup (lid slightly ajar), and reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Stir in the orzo and cook for another 10 minutes or until the orzo is cooked through. I keep the lid off and stir it fairly often because it tends to stick to the bottom of the pot.
  6. Add the chicken to the soup here. Also add the lemon juice (you may want to add more than I suggest - I didn't want to make the soup too lemony for some people), parsley, and season the soup with salt & pepper as needed. Serve immediately.

Recipe Notes:

  • Spicy chicken broth is a must for this recipe, it really makes all the ingredients pop.
  • Cream can be added at the end of the cook to make the soup a bit more creamy if that is what is desired
