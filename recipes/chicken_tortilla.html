Chicken Tortilla Soup

About the Soup:

Chicken Tortilla soup is a great soup on those rainy days where you just want to
watch a movie and have a bowl of something that will keep you warm and full.

This version cooks in the crock pot and can be made in only a few hours on high.
Most of the cooking time is unattended making it a super easy recipe. This also
uses a rotissere chicken making the whole process very quick.

Time to Cook:

4 hours on high

Ingredents:

  • Tortilla Strips
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic large, minced
  • 1 jalapeño diced and seeded
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 14 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can rotel
  • 1 box of spicy chicken broth
  • 14 oz black beans
  • 14 oz can of corn
  • 1 rotissere chicken
  • Bunch of cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Cook onion, garlic and jalapeño in olive oil. Add to the bottom of a slow cooker.
  2. Add remaining ingredients (except tortillas and oil) to the slow cooker and cook on high for 4 hours.
  3. Shred chicken and add to the pot. Serve with tortilla strips.
